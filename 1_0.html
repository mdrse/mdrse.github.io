About me

I was born in 1971 which at the time of writing makes me 48. I was born Neuro-A-Typical(Autistic) but did not know until a few years ago. Being Autistic prior to diagnosis has caused a huge amount of problems in my life, for example I am a suicide survivor at 15, hence, the semicolon necklace, however, since diagnosis I have done a huge amount of research, had a great deal of help and I am continuing a path of personal growth. Autism has caused a number of deficits but I am discovering has gifted me a number of strengths also which I am exploring.

IT was my first love and I had a Commodore 64 at 15, justice which translated to law is my second small obsession and i have a minor interest in psychology as a doorway to understanding myself better.

The earliest training I received was when I was around 18 in COBOL then C programming.

2001 I was an IT Manager for a car sales business, it was a very stressful job and the owner was a martinet, a close friend died and my brain did the equivalent of a blue screen of death, I then isolated myself in my fortress of solitude(my flat) and eat(self harmed) my way to 38 stone in weight or 241.311KG.

I had a two stage duodenal switch operation in 2009 & 2010, this has reduced my weight to its current level of around 20 stone or 127.006KG. I hope to eventually reach around 15 stone which is a normal weight for my height and build.

The upsides are I can eat without putting on weight, it is impossible for me to become diabetic. The downsides are I have to obtain 100g of protein each day from my meals & I have to take a large number of supplements daily and when I do reach my goal weight I will need to have further surgeries on skin.

2015 I was coming out of my depression and was thinking: “What do I want to do next?”. I did a GCSE Maths & English with a view to going on a 1 year access to higher education course in Law & Criminology, a family member in 2016 asked me to help them with a landlord and tenant issue which lasted till April 2018 so I never continued with that plan, I consider it on hold, I still hope to study law, as law is a love but the system of law is not. I got by helping the family member a peek behind the curtain at the system of law in England & Wales and gained some on the job training as I like to call it and I now have some skills.

I used my newly gained law knowledge to navigate the benefits system for myself and now volunteer with Dial House in Chester where I write court submissions for lower tribunal to help people get their benefits back when the benefits have been unjustly removed, I also do this privately for people who approach me which I do not charge for only recoup expenses such as postage.

I am active in the Chester Labour Party and was a paper candidate for Tarpoley at the last local election after which I was elected as “Disability Officer” on Chester Constituency Labour Party’s Executive Committee.

2018 I was invited to be a “Community Inspirer”(CI) on the 2nd West Cheshire Poverty Truth Commission, there are 15 CIs out of a population of around 300,000 in West Cheshire so it is a responsibility and an honour to be asked and I take the role very seriously. Why did I join the Code Nation Coding Course?

During the two years I was helping my family member I also started developing a business idea heart-is.co.uk Ltd, this was produced by developers but I’d like to re-write this from scratch in JavaScript to have a deep understanding of the project before moving it forward.

The purpose of training with Code Nation is to build on my existing IT skills with 2020 industry standard practices so I can add further options to consider, by doing both the 3 week and the 3 month course I would be able to consider if I wanted to work for an employer in the future or continue with my business idea. “Education creates options which creates freedom!”.
